Butter Chicken

Calories: 350 kcal

Health Benefits: Rich in healthy fats, high in fiber, great source of protein and vitamins.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (boneless, cut into bite-sized pieces) 1/2 cup yogurt 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste 1 tbsp red chili powder 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala Salt to taste Juice of 1 lemon 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • For the gravy: 2 tbsp butter (plus extra for cooking) 1 medium onion (finely chopped) 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste 1 tsp cumin seeds 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder 1/2 tsp turmeric powder 1 cup tomato puree (or blended tomatoes) 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup water (adjust to consistency) Salt to taste Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish) 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves, optional)

Instructions:

  2. Marinate the chicken pieces in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, salt, lemon juice, and vegetable oil. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a pan with butter and cook the marinated chicken until browned and cooked through. Set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add more butter and saut√© the chopped onions until golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste, cumin seeds, garam masala, red chili powder, and turmeric powder. Cook until fragrant.
  6. Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  7. Stir in the heavy cream and water. Adjust the consistency with more water if needed.
  8. Season with salt and add the cooked chicken pieces. Simmer for a few minutes to let the flavors meld.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and crushed kasuri methi. Serve hot with rice or naan.
