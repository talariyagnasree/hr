Idli and Sambar

Calories: 350 kcal

Health Benefits: Rich in healthy fats, high in fiber, great source of protein and vitamins.

Idli:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice (preferably parboiled rice)
  • 1/4 cup split urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds (optional)
  • Water (for soaking and grinding)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Oil (for greasing)

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice,fenugreek seeds and dal seperately for 5 hours.
  2. Grind soaked rice and dal seperately and add water when needed.
  3. Ferment the batter which id made from mixing the rice and dal batter overnight.
  4. Add salt and steam it with little oil in small molds.
  5. Let them cool for a few minutes and serve with sambar and coconut chutney!
