Khichidi

Calories: 350 kcal

Health Benefits: Rich in healthy fats, high in fiber, great source of protein and vitamins.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rice 1/4 cup yellow moong dal (split yellow lentils) 1 medium onion (chopped) 1 small tomato (chopped) 1-2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste 1/2 tsp cumin seeds 1/2 tsp turmeric powder 1/2 tsp garam masala (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp asafoetida (hing) 1 tbsp ghee or oil Salt to taste 4 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak rice and moong dal for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pressure cooker and add cumin seeds.
  3. Add chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste. Saute until onions are translucent.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, garam masala, and asafoetida. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Drain the soaked rice and dal and add them to the pressure cooker.
  6. Add water and salt. Mix well and pressure cook for 2-3 whistles.
  7. Once the pressure is released, open the cooker, fluff the khichdi with a fork, and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with yogurt, pickle, or papad.
