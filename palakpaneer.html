Palak Paneer

Calories: 350 kcal

Health Benefits: Rich in healthy fats, high in fiber, great source of protein and vitamins.

Ingredients:

    Ingredients: For the Palak (Spinach) Puree: Water (for blanching)
  • 4 cups fresh spinach (palak), washed and chopped 1-2 green chilies (optional, for heat) 1-inch ginger piece 1 garlic clove (optional)
  • For the Gravy: 2 tbsp oil or ghee 1 medium onion, finely chopped 1 medium tomato, pureed or chopped 1 tsp cumin seeds 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder 1 tsp garam masala 1/2 tsp cumin powder 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste 1/2 cup water (adjust for desired gravy consistency) 200g paneer, cut into cubes 1/4 cup cream (optional, for richness)

Instructions:

  1. For the Palak Puree: Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add the chopped spinach and blanch for 2-3 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process.
  2. Transfer the blanched spinach to a blender. Add green chilies, ginger, and garlic. Blend into a smooth puree. Set aside.
  3. For the Gravy: Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add the finely chopped onion and saut√© until golden brown.
  5. Stir in the tomato puree or chopped tomato. Cook until the oil separates.
  6. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, cumin powder, and coriander powder. Mix well.
